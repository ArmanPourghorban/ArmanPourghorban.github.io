About

I’m Arman Pourghorban, a Controls Engineer at Tesla and a PhD candidate in control systems & robotics. My research spans model-predictive control, dynamics-aware trajectory optimization, and game-theoretic target defense for multi-agent systems. I enjoy translating rigorous theory into robust, testable software for real machines.

Model Predictive Control Trajectory Optimization Multi-Agent Systems Planning & Perception Optimization & Reinforcement Learning ROS • MATLAB • Python

Selected Projects

  • Target–Defender–Intruder Games — Engagement surfaces, capture analysis, and Markov-chain abstractions for sequential attacker arrivals.
  • Trajectory Optimization for 6-DOF Robots — MPC and dynamics-aware path planning with verification and safety constraints.
  • Flexible Assignment Guard — Centralized defender coordination vs. decentralized fixed-sector guards, showing provable improvements in capture rates.
